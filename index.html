🎓 Understanding Compound Interest

Compound interest is when you earn interest not just on your original money, but also on the interest you've already earned. It's like a snowball rolling downhill - it starts small but gets bigger and bigger!

The Rule of 72: Divide 72 by your interest rate to see how long it takes to double your money. At 7%, your money doubles every ~10 years!

Time is your friend: Starting early is more powerful than contributing more later. Even small amounts can grow into life-changing wealth over time.
